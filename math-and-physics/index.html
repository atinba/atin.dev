A kinda cool proof that sum of roots of unity is zero

$$p(x) = x^{n-1} + x^{n-2} + \cdots + x + 1 $$ $$x p(x) = x^n + x^{n-1} + \cdots + x^2 + x $$ $$p(x) = \frac{x^n-1}{x-1}$$ $$p(\omega) = \omega^{n-1} + \cdots + \omega + 1 = \frac{\omega^n-1}{\omega-1} = 0$$
